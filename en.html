What AEO actually is

AEO is the engineering side of being mentioned by AI assistants. Schema.org markup, machine-readable metadata, canonical entity definitions, consistent business identity across data sources, content that maps cleanly to how questions are asked.

It is roughly to AI assistants what SEO was to Google in 2005: a set of structural fixes that take a site from "the algorithm cannot tell what this is" to "the algorithm has enough signal to cite it confidently."

We are a small team, based in Stavanger, Norway. Most of our day-to-day is Norwegian SMB work. The methodology is language-agnostic, and we have shipped foundations for English-language SaaS clients on the same basis.

How we work

  • Audit. We score the site (0–100), test what AI assistants currently say about you when asked, and write up the gaps.
  • Foundation. Schema, content structure, machine-readable metadata, canonical entity, business profile, llms.txt, agent-card.
  • Monthly monitoring. Visibility tracking, content updates, and quarterly review of what shifted in the AI assistants you care about.
